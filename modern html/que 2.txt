HTML entities are special codes used to represent reserved characters and symbols in HTML. These codes ensure that certain characters are displayed correctly in web browsers, even if they have a special meaning in HTML or could be misinterpreted.

< - less than
Represents the less-than sign (<).

> - greater than
Represents the greater-than sign (>).

& - ampersand
Represents the ampersand (&).

" - double quotation mark
Represents the double quotation mark (").

' - single quotation mark (apostrophe)
Represents the single quotation mark (').
